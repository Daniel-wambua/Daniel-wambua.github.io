Why Join Bug Bounty Programs?

Ethical hacking offers numerous benefits beyond financial rewards. Here's what you can expect:

Earn Money

Get paid for finding vulnerabilities, with bounties ranging from $100 to $100,000+ for critical discoveries.

Build Reputation

Establish yourself in the security community and get invited to private programs with higher rewards.

Learn & Grow

Sharpen your skills by testing real-world systems and learning from other researchers.

Career Opportunities

Many bug bounty hunters get recruited by top tech companies for security positions.
